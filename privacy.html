Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 22, 2025

This privacy policy outlines how the Lumnia Discord bot collects, uses, and protects information obtained through your interaction with the bot.

1. Information We Collect

  • Discord User ID, Username, and Discriminator
  • Server ID and Channel ID where interactions occur
  • Message activity metrics (counts, timestamps)
  • Voice channel activity durations
  • Coin balances, XP, and level progress
  • Daily login and task completions

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data to:

  • Track your activity and provide rewards (coins, XP, roles)
  • Show leaderboard statistics
  • Deliver customized content such as market offers and daily missions

3. Data Storage & Protection

All data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and access control. No sensitive data (such as passwords, messages, or email addresses) is collected.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties. Data is only used internally to enhance your experience with the bot.

5. Your Rights

If you wish to have your data removed, you can request deletion via our support channels.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as necessary. All changes will be reflected on this page with a new effective date.
